About Us

How Travel Agency Work

A travel agency acts as a middleman between travelers and travel service providers. It helps clients book flights, hotels, transportation, and vacation packages. Travel agencies offer personalized itineraries, handle visa processes, provide travel insurance, and suggest the best destinations based on preferences and budget. They earn through commissions from airlines, hotels, and tour operators or charge service fees. Modern agencies operate both online and offline, ensuring hassle-free travel planning.
